Ingredients:
 - 1.5 cups of cooked and cooled rice
 - 1 egg beaten
 - 1 tbsp soy sauce
 - 150g of sliced meat of your choice
 - 2 teaspoons oil

Directions:
 1. Heat pan on high
 2. Add 2 teaspoons of oil
 3. Add 1.5 cups of rice to pan and stir until the rice has separated
 4. Once the rice is loose add 150g of meat and stir
 5. Add 1 beaten egg and stir so the egg is scambled
 6. Add 1 tbsp of soy sauce
 7. Continue to stir until you see the rice pop from the pan, once this happens remove from heat and serve
